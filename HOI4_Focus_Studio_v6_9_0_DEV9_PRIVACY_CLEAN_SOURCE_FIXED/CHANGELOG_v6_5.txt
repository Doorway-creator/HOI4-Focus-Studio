HOI4 Focus Studio v6.5 - National Spirits

- Added a National Spirits tab.
- Create named national spirits with descriptions, custom IDs and raw HOI4 modifiers.
- Choose an image directly from your files; Studio stores it in the project and exports a centered 64x64 DDS icon.
- Link each spirit to a focus that grants it and optionally a focus that removes it.
- Export generates common/ideas, interface sprite definitions, localisation and add_ideas/remove_ideas focus rewards.
- Lossless project sidecar preserves all national spirit data on re-import.
