HOI4 Focus Studio v3

- Shows the actual HOI4 focus icons instead of generic stars.
- Mouse-wheel zoom centers on the cursor.
- Hold the left mouse button on empty space and drag to pan around the tree.
- Hold the left mouse button on a focus and drag it to move it.
- Right-click empty space to place a new focus at the cursor.
- Right-click a focus to connect it, replace its icon, or attach an event.
- Custom icons can also be dragged directly onto a focus.
- Added normal country-event and newspaper news-event previews.
- Event pictures, titles, descriptions, options, triggers, and effects are editable.
- Events attached to focuses are written into completion_reward during export.

Controls
--------
Mouse wheel: zoom in or out
Left-drag empty background: move around the tree
Left-drag focus: move focus
Right-click background: place focus / connect
Right-click focus: connections / icons / events
