HOI4 Focus Studio v5 - Decisions, Characters, and Technology

- Added a Decisions editor tab.
- Decisions can be standalone or unlocked by a selected focus.
- Decision settings: name, description, PP cost, active/countdown days,
  political-power reward, one-time manpower addition/removal, and temporary or
  permanent recruitable-manpower modifiers.
- Multiple decisions can be attached to the same focus.
- Added a Characters editor tab.
- Characters support imported portraits, names, IDs, skill, and General,
  Admiral, or military high-command Officer roles.
- Characters can be recruited by a focus or a decision.
- Added technology rewards to the focus reward builder.
- Technology can be researched/unlocked immediately or receive a one-use
  research bonus.
- Export now generates decision categories, decisions, character definitions,
  portrait DDS files, permanent decision ideas, focus unlocks, and localisation.
